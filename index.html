Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. When you click the crytsals you will add a specific amount of pionts to your total score.

To win the game you must match your score to the random number. If your go over the random number you will lose!

The value of each of the crytals are hidden from you until they are clicked.

Everytime the game restarts the value of each crystal will change.
